FAQ

Questions we hear most.

What is ATOM AI, exactly?
ATOM AI is an autonomous sales operating system. It runs the full motion — targeting buyers, dialing live with an AI voice rep, briefing every account, and pushing deals to close — from a single workspace.
Does ATOM replace my SDR team?
No — ATOM amplifies them. It absorbs the repetitive top‑of‑funnel work so reps spend their time on qualified, high‑intent conversations. Customers typically redeploy SDR capacity into deeper account work, not headcount cuts.
How does the AI voice rep sound?
Natural and emotion‑aware. Chris responds in under a second, handles common objections, and books meetings on your calendar. Every call is transcribed, scored, and synced to your CRM.
What about compliance and data security?
ATOM is multi‑tenant by design, AES‑256 encryption at rest and in transit, SSO‑ready, and built with TCPA, DNC, GDPR, and CCPA workflows. SOC 2 audit is in progress.
